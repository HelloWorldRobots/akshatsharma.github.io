Arduino based 4WD with automated Face-Cam Tracking (independent servo controlled) using Casscade Classifers with Raspberry Pi and Picam
Arduino‑powered 4‑wheel‑drive robotic platform integrated with a Raspberry Pi and PiCam for real‑time face tracking. The system uses OpenCV cascade classifiers to detect faces and independently actuated servos to orient the camera toward the target. Features include autonomous tracking behavior, synchronized motor control, and a modular hardware/software architecture for future expansion.
